# VirtualFit AI

## Overview

VirtualFit AI is an AI-powered virtual fashion platform that allows users to upload a full-body image and digitally try on clothing before purchasing.

The platform combines computer vision, deep learning, and modern web technologies to provide an interactive shopping experience.

---

## Problem Statement

Online shoppers cannot accurately visualize how clothes will look on them, leading to poor purchasing decisions and high product return rates.

---

## Proposed Solution

VirtualFit AI enables users to:

- Upload their image
- Select clothing
- Generate realistic virtual try-on results using AI
- Receive personalized outfit recommendations

---

## Target Users

- Online shoppers
- Fashion brands
- Clothing retailers
- Fashion influencers

---

## Goals

- Improve online shopping experience
- Reduce clothing returns
- Provide personalized styling recommendations
